Our Mission

The UA Film Society is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of film enthusiasts and promoting cinema culture on campus. Our mission is to:

  • Celebrate the art of filmmaking and storytelling by showcasing a diverse range of films from around the world.
  • Provide opportunities for students to explore, learn, and engage with films through screenings, discussions, workshops, and events.
  • Offer educational resources and support to aspiring filmmakers, including access to equipment, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities.
  • Promote inclusivity and diversity in the film industry by amplifying underrepresented voices and stories.
